# About Us - Trades Center

About TRADES CENTER

## A New Way to Renovate

**Hello to everyone in the Tradescenter community!**  
  
Hey everyone, Glad to see you here. My name is **David** and let me tell you a bit about myself. As an employee, starting my own business was always on the back of my mind, but one of the big questions was "how would I get my own work?". This led me to ask other small businesses about where and how much they paid for advertising services. The responses I got made it clear that there had to be a better and more affordable way for companies to advertise. Growing a business is tough, and wasting large amounts of money on advertising makes it even tougher. That's why I put Tradescenter together. Affordable advertising pricing for all businesses - no matter the size, and a 'Get Inspired' section designed to help viewers to get ideas for home renovation projects. Tradescenter will help alleviate the high cost of advertising while providing a simple experience for home owners to get inspired, and hire the right company.  
  
Trades Center was created for consumers to view local work being done in their area and rate local business’s based on their dealings with the business. We offer a showcase section for business’s to post their projects for viewing and a customer review system to base their work on.  
  
Our main focus is to let companies showcase their work and give consumers ideas for their projects while giving the consumer the chance to publicly rate the companies on their performance. These real reviews give consumers the best chance at getting the job done right the first time. The Trades Center team understands referrals are the #1 source of business, and we believe that these reviews will serve as a collection of referrals and will help local businesses flourish.
